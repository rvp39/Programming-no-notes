





+===========+=================+===========+=========================================+
|  Name     |      path       |  Http req | purpose                                 |
+===========+=================+===========+=========================================+
|Index      | /Blogs          | GET       | List all the Blogs                      |
+-----------+-----------------+-----------+-----------------------------------------+
|New        | /Blogs/new      | GET       | Show new blog form                      |
+-----------+-----------------+-----------+-----------------------------------------+
|Create     | /Blogs          | POST      | Create a new blog then redirect to Index|
+-----------+-----------------+-----------+-----------------------------------------+
|Show       | /Blogs/:id      | GET       | Show one blog                           |
+-----------+-----------------+-----------+-----------------------------------------+
|Edit       | /Blogs/:id/edit |GET        | Show edit form for one Blogs            |
+-----------+-----------------+-----------+-----------------------------------------+
|Update     | /Blogs/:id      | PUT       |  update ablog then redirect somewhere   |
+-----------+-----------------+-----------+-----------------------------------------+
|Destroy    | /Blogs/:id      | DELETE    | Deletes a Blog                          |
+-----------+-----------------+-----------+-----------------------------------------+
